Help improve the HCA DCP

The HCA DCP product team invites you to participate in a user experience study on the usability of the Data Portal, the DCP matrix file formats, and metadata.

To participate, please take a short survey to tell us a bit about yourself, your work, and how you use the matrices available from the Data Portal.

Use the survey form to indicate if you are available to participate in a 15 - 20 minute virtual interview about your experiences finding and using matrices. For more information, read the Spring 2021 DCP Matrix UX Study overview.
